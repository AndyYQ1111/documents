# VaultKey – App Store Privacy Summary

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

This summary is provided for App Store review purposes and gives a clear, concise overview of VaultKey’s privacy practices.

---

## Overview

VaultKey is a **fully offline password manager**. The app does not require an internet connection and does not operate any backend servers. All data is processed and stored locally on the user’s device.

---

## Data Collection

**VaultKey does not collect any user data.**

Specifically, the app:

* Does **not** collect personal information (name, email address, phone number)
* Does **not** collect identifiers (device ID, advertising ID)
* Does **not** collect location data
* Does **not** collect usage analytics or tracking data
* Does **not** use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

---

## User-Provided Data

Any information entered by the user (such as passwords, usernames, or notes):

* Is created voluntarily by the user
* Is stored **only on the user’s device**
* Is encrypted locally
* Is never transmitted off the device

VaultKey has **no access** to this data.

---

## Biometric & Authentication Data

* Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) is handled entirely by the operating system
* VaultKey does **not** access, store, or process raw biometric data
* PIN codes and authentication results are stored securely on-device

---

## Data Sharing

VaultKey does **not** share data with any third parties, including:

* Advertisers
* Analytics providers
* Cloud services
* Data brokers

---

## Data Storage & Retention

* All data is stored locally using system secure storage (iOS Keychain / Secure Enclave)
* Data remains on the device until the user deletes it
* Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data

---

## Tracking

VaultKey does **not** track users across apps or websites and does **not** engage in any form of tracking as defined by Apple.

---

## Compliance Statement

VaultKey is designed to comply with Apple App Store privacy requirements, including:

* App Tracking Transparency (ATT)
* Privacy Nutrition Label disclosures
* On-device data processing principles
